How did it started?




The COVID 19 was first reported in Wuhan China. It was first
identified in december 2019 and has resulted to an ongoing
pandemic. It initially occured in a group of people with severe
disease like pneumonia. This disease has been spread from the
sick to others inculding family members and health care staff
such as nurse and doctor or the so called frontliners. The virus
is yet to be determined of how this virus spread. In general
respiratory its usually transmitted through droplets that created
when an infected person cough or sneeze.
