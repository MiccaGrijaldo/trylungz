How did it spread?


The Covid-19 is a disease that widely spread in just a little amount of time. In just a year, it already took millions of lives and turned our world upside down. But how does this disease spread? Covid-19 is a disease that can be transmitted. If you will be in contact with a person who has this disease, it can be easily transmitted into you. The virus is transmitted by droplets such as saliva and tears. They are also studying if the said virus is an airborne or not. The virus spread widely as there are a lot of people who does not follow the health protocols. As the people get closer to each other, the bigger the chances that there is a virus transmitter among the group that can be transmitted to the people around that person, with that being said, the virus fastly spreads.
