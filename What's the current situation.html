What's the current situation?


The world has considered COVID a dangerous threat. Because of
this, new rules have been implemented to prevent the spreading.
Businesses, social interaction, and even going to school are
prohibited. But some people are still spreading the virus although
they didn't mean it. Unlike the cases from the past 10 months, the
cases had luckily decreased. But, it's not yet for us to celebrate.
New variants of Corona Virus have been identified in different
countries which are more dangerous.
